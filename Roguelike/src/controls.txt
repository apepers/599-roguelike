Severed Space Controls:

q - quit
, - pickup
i - inventory
e - eat
d - drop
l - look (at ground)
w - wield (weapon)
p - put on (armor)
o - open door
s - shut door
> - go up stairs
< - go down stairs
c - center map
? - identify
enter - select (in menus and cursor)
arrow keys - movement
spacebar - wait
